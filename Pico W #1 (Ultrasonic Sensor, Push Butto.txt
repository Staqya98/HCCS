Pico W #1 (Ultrasonic Sensor, Push Button, Buzzer, LED):

Connect the ultrasonic sensor to Pico W #1:
VCC of the ultrasonic sensor to 3V3 on Pico W #1.
GND of the ultrasonic sensor to GND on Pico W #1.
Trig of the ultrasonic sensor to GP0 on Pico W #1.
Echo of the ultrasonic sensor to GP1 on Pico W #1.
Connect the push button to Pico W #1:
Connect one terminal of the push button to GP2 on Pico W #1.
Connect the other terminal of the push button to GND on Pico W #1.
Connect the buzzer to Pico W #1:
Connect the positive terminal of the buzzer to GP3 on Pico W #1.
Connect the negative terminal of the buzzer to GND on Pico W #1.
Connect an LED to Pico W #1:
Connect the anode (longer leg) of the LED to GP4 on Pico W #1.
Connect the cathode (shorter leg) of the LED to GND on Pico W #1.
